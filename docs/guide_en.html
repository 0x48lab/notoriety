Notoriety

Reputation System Player Guide

What is the Notoriety System?

Notoriety is a reputation system where your actions determine your standing. Good players are protected, while those who commit crimes face penalties.

Blue (Innocent)

Law-abiding player

  • Trade with villagers at normal prices
  • Your property is protected
  • Keep items on death
  • Golems are neutral

Gray (Criminal)

Criminal

  • Villager prices are 1.5x higher
  • No ownership on new placements
  • No penalty for being attacked
  • Keep items on death

Red (Murderer)

Murderer

  • Villagers refuse to trade
  • Golems attack on sight
  • Bounty can be placed on you
  • Drop items on death

State Transitions

Blue to Gray

Committing crimes decreases your Alignment. When it drops below 0, you become gray.

Gray to Blue

Time (+10/hour) and good deeds restore Alignment. When it reaches 0 or above, you return to blue.

Becoming Red

Killing a blue player increases your PK count, turning you red.

Warning: You cannot go directly from red to blue. You must first go through a gray period.

Crimes and Penalties

Action Penalty Notes
Kill villager (with bed) -50 Villager linked to a bed
Steal from player's container -50 Taking items from chests
Kill Iron Golem -50 Nearby golems become hostile
Destroy player's property -10 Cancel within 5 sec by replacing
Attack another player -1 Attacking blue players
Attack villager -1 Even without killing
Kill animals -1 When witnessed by villager/golem
Kill villager (no bed) -1 Villager not linked to a bed
Tip: Blocks you placed yourself, or blocks placed by trusted players, can be freely modified.

Trust System

Trusted players can access your property without committing crimes.

  • Open chests and take items
  • Break blocks
  • Attack without it being a crime
Note: Trust is one-directional. Just because you trust someone doesn't mean they trust you.

Bounty System

Red players (murderers) can have bounties placed on them.

  • Minimum amount: 100
  • Multiple bounties on the same player stack
  • Killing a red player claims all bounties
  • Additional XP reward (50-500 based on PK count)
